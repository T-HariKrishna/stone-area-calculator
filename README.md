# Stone Area Calculator

## Overview

Stone Area Calculator is a web-based application designed to simplify stone load calculations and report management. It enables users to enter load quantities, calculate total area instantly, save reports, and access historical records for future reference.

---

## Features

### User Information Management

* Vehicle Number
* Driver Name
* Mining Owner Name
* Mining Owner Phone Number
* Factory Owner Name

### Area Calculation

The calculator includes predefined stone size combinations for quick and accurate calculations:

* 2.5 × 2
* 3 × 1.5, 3 × 2, 3 × 2.5
* 3.5 × 1.5, 3.5 × 2, 3.5 × 2.5
* 4 × 1.5, 4 × 2, 4 × 2.5
* 4.5 × 1.5, 4.5 × 2, 4.5 × 2.5
* 5 × 1.5, 5 × 2, 5 × 2.5
* 5.5 × 1.5, 5.5 × 2, 5.5 × 2.5
* 6 × 1.5, 6 × 2, 6 × 2.5
* 6.5 × 1.5, 6.5 × 2, 6.5 × 2.5
* 7 × 1.5, 7 × 2, 7 × 2.5

### Smart Calculation View

* Automatic total area calculation.
* After clicking **Calculate Total**, rows with zero or empty quantities are automatically removed from the calculation summary.
* Only rows containing actual load quantities are displayed.
* Provides a clean and organized output for easier verification and reporting.

### Report Management

* Save load reports.
* View report history.
* Download reports.
* Delete reports.
* Track calculation date and time.
* Store vehicle and owner details along with calculation results.

### Responsive Design

* Mobile-friendly interface.
* Optimized layout for desktop and mobile devices.
* Compact history card view.
* Smooth and modern user experience.

---

## How It Works

1. Enter vehicle and owner information.
2. Fill in quantities for the required stone sizes.
3. Click **Calculate Total**.
4. The system calculates the total area automatically.
5. Empty or zero-quantity rows are removed from the result view for better clarity.
6. Save the report for future reference.
7. Access saved reports from the History section.
8. Download or delete reports when needed.
